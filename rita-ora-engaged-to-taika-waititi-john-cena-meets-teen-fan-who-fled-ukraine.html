Rita Ora engaged to Taika Waititi; John Cena meets teen fan who fled Ukraine

by Daniel Falconer |
8 June 2022

The past 24-hours have been packed full of celebrity news and gossip, including the latest on Love Island and one celebrity engagement that is turning everybody’s heads! Here are some of the top headlines, in today’s Social Buzz!

Rita Ora engaged to Taika Waititi

Rita Ora and Taika Waititi are engaged / Picture Credit: PA Images/Alamy Stock Photo

Rita Ora and Taika Waititi are said to be planning their wedding, with hopes of saying their vows as soon as possible in a low-key ceremony so that their relationship can be made official.

Rumours suggested that Waititi popped the question over the Christmas period, whilst sources say that the pair are simply looking for a break in their busy schedules so they can tie the knot. They’ll have a low-key ceremony, before hosting a bigger party later this year.

The insider added: “There are a few details to iron out, but it’s coming soon and it’ll be beautifully intimate and perfectly them.”

John Cena meets teen who fled Ukraine

WWE Superstar John Cena has met up with a young teen who fled Ukraine. The refugee was told by his mother that they were on their way to meet his wrestling hero to inspire him to get out of the country, which was invaded by Russia over 100 days ago.

Misha Rohozhyn is 19 and has Down’s syndrome, and was forced out of Mariupol when the Russian invasion started. Now, his dreams of meeting Cena have come true, as the sports legend and Peacemaker actor was left inspired by his story.

Cena said: “Having three days off from work right at the time I read the story, and being an hour away [from the Netherlands] by air, it turned into ‘We’re going.’ And that means spending an afternoon building blocks, eating cake – that’s a special afternoon when it comes to new friends I was able to meet.”

Johnny Depp’s lawyer Camille Vasquez gets promotion

Camille Vasquez shot to fame after representing Johnny Depp in his defamation case against ex-wife Amber Heard and now, the lawyer has been promoted to partner at the law firm Brown Rudnick.

The company announced the promotion in a press release, noting that her performance in the recent high-profile trial was the reason behind the decision. Chairman and CEO William Baldiga, said that they were “incredibly proud of her and look forward to what she will accomplish as our newest partner.”

34-year-old Vasquez said she looks forward to “represent Brown Rudnick’s culture of excellence.”

Love Island viewers want minimum age raised

Love Island 2022 is underway / Picture Credit: ITV/Lifted Entertainment

Some of those watching this year’s series of Love Island have criticised some of the casting decisions, especially after 27-year-old Davide decided to couple up with 19-year-old Gemma.

The youngster, who is the daughter of football legend Michael Owen, had previously said she had been single for a year-and-a-half, meaning that her last relationship would have been when she was just 17. It was a strange topic of conversation on a show where the minimum age to join is 18.

Some took to Twitter to ask for the minimum age requirement on the show to be raised to 21, with one fan in particular saying: “I don’t want to see a 19-year-old kissing someone who’s pushing 30.”
