Bill Burr’s net worth

Bill Burr net worth is estimated to be $20 million as of 2023.

He amassed his fortune through his comedy career, including tours, TV, and podcasting.

Born in 1968 in Canton, Massachusetts, he attributes his unique perspectives to his upbringing.

He earned a reputation for his intelligence and wit as a comedian.

Moving to Los Angeles in the mid-1990s, he appeared on shows like “Chappelle’s Show,” “Breaking Bad,” and “New Girl.”

Burr released successful stand-up specials such as “I’m Sorry You Feel That Way” and “Paper Tiger.”

He hosts “The Monday Morning Podcast” and makes appearances on shows like Joe Rogan’s and Mike Tyson’s.

With his wealth, he indulges in high-end purchases and upgrades his home.

Burr has openly discussed his struggles with anxiety and compulsive gambling, seeking therapy and attending Gamblers Anonymous meetings.

Early life

William Frederick Burr was born in Canton, Massachusetts, on June 10, 1968, to Linda and Robert Burr.

He’s of German and Irish descent and graduated from high school in 1987.

In 1993, he earned a radio bachelor’s degree from Emerson College in Boston, where David Foster Wallace was his professor.

Before comedy, he worked in warehouses and liked the freedom it offered.

Personal Life

Bill Burr married Nia Hill in 2013. They welcomed a daughter in January 2017 and a son in June 2020.

In his spare time, Burr is a licensed helicopter pilot and plays the drums.

He’s a fan of heavy metal music and cigars, even enjoying cigars with Jerry Seinfeld on “Comedians in Cars Getting Coffee.”
