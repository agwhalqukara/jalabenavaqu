Dwayne Johnson Puts His Arm Tattoos On Display While Heading To A Workout

Dwayne Johnson is all smiles while arriving for a workout on Monday afternoon (August 2) in Los Angeles.

The 49-year-old Jungle Cruise star hit the gym for his daily fitness routine and was spotted a few days later hitting the gym once again.

Dwayne‘s gym sessions come just a few days after he explained why he’s not sporting six pack abs.

In another recent interview, Dwayne‘s producing partner, Hiram Garcia, opened up about if there was ever a possibility he could’ve starred in a Marvel film.

Click inside to see what his answer was!

Hiram told Collider “DJ and Kevin [Feige] have a great relationship and we’ve flirted around a few ideas before, but nothing serious.”

“There’s a mutual admiration for what we each have been doing and what Kevin has built over there is truly incredible.”

Dwayne is set to star as the title character in DC’s Black Adam, and that big screen adaptation has been the main focus.

“Ultimately though for us, we’ve always been very committed to what we wanted to build with Black Adam,” Hiram added. “Black Adam was the character we wanted to bring to life and who we felt DJ was destined to play. Once we set our sites on this character over a decade ago it was hard to imagine DJ [playing] anyone else.”
