Jana Kramer Reveals Shes So Proud Of Mike Caussin For Admitting His Addictions

Just a couple of days after Mike Caussin’s shocking announcement, his wife, Jana Kramer, came out in support of her man in the midst of his very personal revelation.

On the 25th of March, the couple spoke candidly on Jana’s iHeartRadio podcast, Whine Down, in which the pair spoke about his sixty-day stay at a treatment facility for sex addiction three years ago.

The ex-NFL player said that Kramer gave him an “ultimatum” when she found out about his cheating ways. As it was previously reported, Jana and Mike called off their marriage of one year after Jana found out Mike cheated on her with another woman.

However, about a year and a half later, Mike and Jana renewed their vows on a beach in Hawaii. Caussin said on the podcast that he has been in a 12-step sex addiction program. Mike isn’t the only person to struggle with an illness as specific as this one.
