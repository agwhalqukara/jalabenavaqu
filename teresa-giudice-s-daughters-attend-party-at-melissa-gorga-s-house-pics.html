Teresa Giudice's Daughters Attend Party at Melissa Gorga's House: Pics

Keeping it in the family! As Teresa Giudice continues to serve her 15-month jail sentence, her children are getting plenty of quality time with their tight-knit family. 

On Wednesday, Aug. 12, Audriana, 5, Milania, 9, and Gabriella Giudice, 10, visited Melissa and Joe Gorga’s house to celebrate the 10th birthday of their cousin Antonia Gorga. 

Big sister Gia, 14, was missing from the group, but the cousins still seemed to be having a great time at the party. Birthday girl Antonia received a flower-covered cake with icing decorations of nail polish, lipstick, and a high-heeled shoe.

Earlier in the day, Antonia’s proud mom, Melissa, 36, posted a collage of photos with the little girl on Instagram, writing, “Happy Birthday to my sweet baby girl the Big 10 Today!! I couldn't ask for more in a daughter. Thoughtful, sweet, generous & kind. You are my Best Friend for life #ANTONIAROSE #myonlygirl.”

Though Melissa and Teresa, 43, have had their fair share of differences in the past, Melissa has been publicly supportive of her sister-in-law.

Teresa is set to complete her sentence in December, and will begin filming for The Real Housewives of New Jersey shortly after, an insider exclusively revealed to Us Weekly. 

“She’s definitely back next season,” the source tells Us.
