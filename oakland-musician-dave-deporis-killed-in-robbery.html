Oakland Musician Dave Deporis Killed In Robbery

OAKLAND, CA (CelebrityAccess) — Oakland Singer Songwriter Dave Deporis, 40, was killed while chasing a thief who allegedly stole a laptop holding the results of a recording session in an Oakland studio.

According to the San Francisco Chronicle, Deporis gave pursuit after the alleged thief snatched Deporis's laptop from a cafe table shortly after noon. Deporis gave chase and grabbed on to the doorhandle of a red Audi sport utility vehicle the robber was using to escape, and was dragged a short distance before falling under the vehicle's tires.

Deporis was pronounced dead at a local hospital and police are reportedly searching for a suspect, the Chronicle said.

Deporis was well-regarded in the region's music scene and his death brought an outpouring of condolences from his colleagues.

“I found out that the beautiful musician #DaveDeporis has passed away… I first heard him years ago at Sidewalk Cafe,” Regina Spektor wrote on her Twitter account.
