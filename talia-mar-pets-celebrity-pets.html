Virtual Dogs and Pigs

Talia Mar has often talked about her love for dogs and pigs in her content streams. While her pets were initially virtual, she later did adopted a dog in the “real world.”

Mushu (Dog)

Breed: Chow Chow | Adopted Jan 2023

Miniminter and Talia Mar adopted a Chow Chow named Mushu in January 2023. The couple announced the news for their fans with a YouTube video[1].

Throughout her career, Talia has taken on a variety of roles that have showcased her range and versatility as a performer. Talia was featured on Ariana’s hit song “Thank U, Next” and was also part of the music video, which quickly became a viral sensation. It led to collaborations with popular YouTube stars like Tyler Oakley and Miranda Sings, as well as fellow musicians such as Charlie Puth and Troye Sivan.
