Will Smith, Jada Pinkett Smith Victims of Home Intrusion

While the Smiths are away, a stranger will stay. A 26-year-old woman was charged with trespassing into Will Smith and Jada Pinkett Smith's Calabasas, Calif., home, the Los Angeles County District Attorney's Office told Us Weekly in a press release on Monday, June 22.

Police revealed that the woman, Ariel Archer, was found inside the Smith family's kitchen by a housekeeper on March 21. Archer was charged by the DA's office on Monday for trespassing and refusing to leave private property.

The DA's office told Us in a press release late Monday that Archer pleaded no contest late Monday afternoon for entering the couple's home and remaining on the premises. A retraining order was issued, which prohibits Archer from coming 150 yards from the family's home as well as individual members of the famous Hollywood quartet. 

She was also sentenced to three years of summary probation.

Thankfully, the superstar couple and their kids, Willow and Jaden, were not home at the time of the trespassing.
