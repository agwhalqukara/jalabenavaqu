Ed Sheeran scores 10th UK number one single with 'Bad Habits'

published on September 01, 2024
Global superstar Ed Sheeran has debuted at number one in the UK Singles Chart with his latest release, ‘Bad Habits’. In doing so, Ed has become the first British artist to go straight to number one in the UK this year. Marking the singer-songwriter’s 10th chart topper in the UK, Ed now joins an elite group of just nine artists including The Beatles, Elvis Presley and Take That who have achieved this incredible feat.
