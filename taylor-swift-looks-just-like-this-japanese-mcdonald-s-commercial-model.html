Taylor Swift Looks Just Like this Japanese McDonald's Commercial Model

She's loving it!

At least, this McDonald's model may be, because the Internet has taken notice that she's Taylor Swift's secret twin. The model in question appears in an already popular, resurfaced Japanese commercial from the fast-food chain (uploaded to YouTube in 2006) that's now making headlines for its star's uncanny resemblance to the "Blank Space" singer. 

The model works Ronald McDonald's signature red-orange hair (not to mention a skirted take on his striped ensemble). And yet, when looking closely, it's undeniable: She looks a whole lot like Swift! The two ladies share the same bow-lipped pout, porcelain skin, cat eyes, and sideways smile.

While the pop phenom, 25, has yet to respond, she very well may soon enough, as Swift's been known to point out the viral pop culture moments involving her via Instagram. (Remember those posts about Paddington the Bear, Avicci, and her look-alike fan from just last month?)
