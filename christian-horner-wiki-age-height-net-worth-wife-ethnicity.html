Christian Horner Age & Early Life

Christian Edward Johnston Horner was born on November 16, 1973, in Leamington Spa, Warwickshire, England, UK. Before establishing an agency with Horner’s father to provide components to motor manufacturers in the English Midlands, his grandpa worked as a purchasing manager for the Standard Motor Company in Coventry. In addition, he has two brothers, Guy and Jamie. There are presently no records of his childhood since he has not shared anything. In terms of his scholastic experience, he attended Arnold Lodge School in Leamington before enrolling privately at Warwick School.

Career

Previously, he began his racing career as a race car driver in karts after obtaining a Formula Renault scholarship in 1991. He later raced in the British Formula Renault Championship with Manor Motorsport in 1992. He finished the season as the highest-placed rookie and race winner. Furthermore, in order to concentrate on the growth of the Arden team, Horner retired from driving at the conclusion of the season.

However, after retiring from professional racing at the age of 25, Horner signed a deal with Marc Goossens and Viktor Maslov for the 1999 FIA F3000 season. Furthermore, at the start of the year 1999, Dave Richards’ Prodrive firm purchased a 50% share in Arden on behalf of the Russian oil giant Lukoil. Horner purchased Prodrive’s stake after one season in Arden.

In addition, he was looking for a chance to jump into Formula One but was unable to find one. In January 2005, he was hired to lead the Red Bull Racing team. Another honor was his appointment as an Officer of the Order of the British Empire (OBE) in the 2013 Birthday Honours for services to racing.

Christian Horner Wife & Marriage

Who is Christian Horner’s wife? In prior discussions about his relationship, he married Beverley Allen. Olivia Horner, the couple’s first child, was born at the end of the year 2013. However, their love did not continue and they divorced for personal reasons. He then dated former Spice Girls member Geri Halliwell six months following his divorce from Allen in 2014.

The pair subsequently got engaged on November 11, 2014, and married on May 15, 2015, in Woburn, Bedfordshire. Montague, the couple’s first child, was born in January 2017. He also has a stepdaughter, Bluebell Madonna. The pair are now enjoying a happily married life with no symptoms of divorce.
