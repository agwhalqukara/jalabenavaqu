Did Moriah Plath Leave Her Family? Her Birthday, Age, Siblings, Boyfriend, Facts

The famous TLC show, Welcome to Plathville, which focuses on the lives of the Plath family, has one of the breakout stars in the name of Moriah Plath. She came to the limelight after leaving the family home in the show back in March 2020.

Moriah Plath is a reality television star who was 16 years old when she started filming for the family reality-TV show, Welcome to Plathville. She previously announced her relationship with an athlete boyfriend on her Instagram.

Moriah Plath Bio & Family: Age, Birthday, Siblings

The 18-years-of-age reality-TV star was born as Moriah Jasper Plath in 2002, in the United States. She celebrates her birthday on 28 August every year. Moriah comes from a big Plath family which consists of her parents, Barry Plath and Kim Plath.

Moriah grew up alongside her 4 brothers and 5 sisters. The name of her siblings include brothers: Micah Plath, Ethan Plath, Joshua Plath, and Issac Plath, and sisters: Hosanna Plath, Lydia Plath, Amber Plath, Cassia Plath, and Mercy Plath. Sadly, her eldest brother, Joshua, passed away after suffering a tragic accident when he was just 17. Joshua got in the way of the vehicle that his mother was driving.

All of the other Moriah’s siblings can be seen in the show, Welcome to Plathville. The actress possesses white ethnicity and her birth sign is Virgo.

Her Acting Career: Welcome to Plathville, Net Worth

Moriah is one of the strongest characters of TLC’s reality-TV show, Welcome to Plathville. She is the second oldest daughter in the Plath family and is a kind of a rebel in the family. She is mostly close to her brothers, Micah Plath and Ethan Plath, as well as Ethan’s wife, Olivia Plath.

Talking more about the show, Moriah always had a feud with her parents which got more heated after filming on Season 1 of Welcome to Plathville wrapped. She later decided to leave the family home to move in with her brother Ethan and his wife, Olivia in March 2020. Though leaving her home, she makes contact with her younger siblings.

Apart from Welcome to Plathville, Moriah has another acting credit to her name. She appeared as Brittany in the 2018 short movie titled You Should Be There. The actual net worth of the young actress remains under review as of now.
