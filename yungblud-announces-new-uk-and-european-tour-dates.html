Yungblud announces new UK and European tour dates

Yungblud has announced a 19-date UK and European tour for this Autumn.

The ‘Loner’ star will kick off his tour in Amsterdam on October 27. He will then stop off in cities including Munich, Milan, Warsaw, Oslo and Paris before reaching the UK. Yungblud will play seven UK dates including a huge show at London’s O2 Academy Brixton.

YUNGBLUD UK & EUROPEAN DATES 

Sunday 27th October – Amsterdam, Paradiso 
Monday 28th October – Brussels, AB Main Hall  
Thursday 31st October – Munich, Backstage Werk  
Saturday 2nd November – Milan, Fabrique  
Monday 4th November – Prague, Roxy  
Tuesday 5th November – Warsaw, Proxima 
Thursday 7th November – Berlin, Kesselhaus  
Sunday 10th November – Copenhagen, Amager Bio  
Monday 11th November, Oslo, Parkteateret  
Wednesday 13th November – Hamburg, Docks 
Thursday 14th November – Cologne, Essigfabrik  
Saturday 16th November – Paris, Trabendo  
Tuesday 19th November – Bristol, O2 Academy 
Wednesday 20th November – Manchester, O2 Ritz 
Thursday 21st November – London, O2 Academy Brixton  
Friday 22nd November – Birmingham, O2 Institute 
Sunday 24th November – Norwich, UEA  
Monday 25th November – Glasgow, SWG3 Galvanisers 
Friday 29th November – Sheffield, O2 Academy 

Tickets go on sale at 9am tomorrow (February 6).

2019 is proving to be a great year for Yungblud so far. His recent single ‘Loner’ was premiered on Radio 1’s Indie Show with Jack Saunders and has since racked up over 1.5 million streams on Spotify. The music video sees him return to various locations in his hometown of Doncaster, including his nan’s house!

Yungblud has just completed a sold out European tour, whilst he will be embarking on a sold out UK tour this March. The singer will also be performing on the main stage at Reading & Leeds festival later this year.
