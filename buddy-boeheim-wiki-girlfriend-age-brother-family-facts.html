Buddy Boeheim Wiki: Parents, Brother, Family Life

Boeheim is one of the three children of parents Jim Boeheim and Juli Boeheim. His father Jim, age, 77, is the head coach of his team, the Syracuse Orange.

Jim was inducted into the Basketball Hall of Fame in September 2005. Moreover, his mother Juli is a successful businesswoman and housewife.


Buddy’s parents got married in 1997. He has a twin sister named Jamie Boeheim and an older brother named Jimmy Boeheim. Meanwhile, his siblings have played basketball during their college years.


Jamie played college basketball and served as team manager for sophomore season at the University of Rochester. Similarly, Jimmy played college basketball as a forward at Cornell University from 2017 to 2020.

The 22-year-old Buddy has a half-sister named Elizabeth Boeheim. Elizabeth was adopted by Jim and his ex-wife Elaine Boeheim in 1985.

Pursuing A Career In Basketball

Buddy with a height of 6 feet 6 inches attended Jamesville-DeWitt High School in DeWitt, New York. As a sophomore, he averaged 14.3 points a game. Additionally, he averaged 26.3 points and 9.8 rebounds as a junior.

In his senior season, he enrolled at Brewster Academy in Wolfeboro, New Hampshire. There, he was a team captain for Coach Jason Smith and one of nine players on the roster who signed with Division I programs.


Afterward, Boeheim committed to play college basketball at Syracuse University. As a freshman, he averaged 6.8 points per game and averaged 15.3 points in his sophomore year.
