Natasha Bedingfield shares new track 'Together In This'

published on September 01, 2024
GRAMMY-nominated artist Natasha Bedingfield has unveiled her latest track ‘Together In This’, which is taken from the animated movie Jungle Beat: The Movie. Written by acclaimed singer-songwriter Jonas Myrin, who is also a longtime friend of Natasha’s, ‘Together In This’ is the feel-good anthem we need in our lives, especially during such an unprecedented time. Whilst the track was inspired by the film’s themes of togetherness and friendship, ‘Together In This’ is also so relatable during the current pandemic, reminding us that whilst we may be apart, we’re never alone.
