Amanda Holden fronts JD Williams Womankind ad campaign alongside Davina McCall

AMANDA Holden goes total disco diva in a jumpsuit — and looks ready to head for the dance floor.

The Britain’s Got Talent judge, 50, got her silver glad rags on and is hot to trot ahead of Freedom Day on Monday.

She has signed up to front online retailer JD Williams’ Womankind ad campaign with fellow TV star Davina McCall, 53.

Davina posed in a salmon pink blazer and trousers.

Together, they’re a Womankind of magic.

At the weekend, Amanda shared her support for the England team by posing in just an England shirt.

The telly favourite also attended the final yesterday at Wembley as the Three Lions faced Italy.

Despite a heartbreaking defeat, a host of celebrities have since shared their support for a team who united a nation and made fans feel such euphoric emotions throughout the tournament.

Amanda wrote: “It didn’t come home but oh my god do I feel proud of @england and #GarethSouthgate for last nights match.

“It takes balls to volunteer for penalties. A night never to forget, we came together as a nation and we still have the #worldcup next year….”
